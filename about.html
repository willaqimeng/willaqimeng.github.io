Willa is an actor, writer, director, and visual artist. She was born in Nanchang, Jiangxi and grew up in New York City. She is one of three sisters. She attended Barnard and graduated in 2020 with honors and a BA in English. Her fiction won the Axinn Foundation/Anna Quindlen Award for Creative Writing and has been published in the Columbia Daily Spectator and Asian American Writers' Workshop. She is currently based in Los Angeles earning an MFA in Film & TV Production at USC's School of Cinematic Arts. Her writing and work in film explore adoption, girlhood, Asian American identity, and dreams.
