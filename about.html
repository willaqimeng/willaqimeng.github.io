Willa is an actor, writer, director, and visual artist from New York.

She attended undergrad at Barnard College where she earned a BA in English and Creative Writing. Her senior project won the Anna Quindlen Award for Creative Writing, funded by the Axinn Foundation. Her other writing has been published in Asian American Writers' Workshop.

Currently, she is at the USC School of Cinematic Arts working towards her MFA in Film & TV Production. While at USC, she has focused on sound design, production design and editing. Off campus, she interned at the Sundance Institute's Feature Film Program, where she evaluated scripts for the 2023 Lab and Intensive. She is now a script reader for the 10th edition of The Writers Lab.

Her writing often explores Asian American identity, interracial adoption, and teenhood through a magical realist lens.
