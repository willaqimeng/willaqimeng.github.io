Willa is a writer, filmmaker, and visual artist from New York.

She has a BA in English from Barnard College, where her creative non-fiction won the Axinn Foundation-funded Anna Quindlen Award for Creative Writing. Off-campus, her writing has been published in Asian American Writers' Workshop.

She is now a graduating MFA student at the USC School of Cinematic Arts in Film & TV Production where she wrote and directed her first short, completed her first feature script, and trained in production design and editing.

She has interned at Sundance's 2023 Feature Film Program and is now a script reader for The Writers Lab.

Her work explores family identity, interracial adoption, and feminism.
