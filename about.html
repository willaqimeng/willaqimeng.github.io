WILLA 洪浩齐萌
CUTHRELL-TUTTLEMAN

Willa is an actor, writer, director, and visual artist from New York.
She graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Barnard College in 2020, where she majored in English and concentrated in Creative Writing.
Her senior thesis won the Axinn Foundation/Anna Quindlen Award for Creative Writing. She is now based in Los Angeles, earning her master's degree in Film & TV Production at USC's School of Cinematic Arts.
She has previously worked at the Asian American Writers' Workshop, the Athena Film Festival, and Sundance Institute.
