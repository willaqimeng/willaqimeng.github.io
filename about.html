WILLA 洪浩齐萌
CUTHRELL-TUTTLEMAN

Willa is an actor, writer, director, and visual artist from New York.
She graduated from Barnard College in 2020 with a BA in English. Her senior thesis won the Axinn Foundation/Anna Quindlen Award for Creative Writing, and her non-fiction on Asian American adoption has been published in Columbia Daily Spectator and Asian American Writers' Workshop's The Margins. She is currently based in Los Angeles earning an MFA in Film & TV Production at USC's School of Cinematic Arts.
