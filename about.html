WILLA 洪浩齐萌
CUTHRELL-TUTTLEMAN

Willa is an actor, writer, director, and visual artist from New York.
She graduated from Barnard College in 2020, where she earned her bachelor's degree in English + Creative Writing. Her senior thesis won the Axinn Foundation/Anna Quindlen Award for Creative Writing. She is based in Los Angeles, earning a master's in Film & TV Production at USC's School of Cinematic Arts.
She has worked at the Asian American Writers' Workshop, the Athena Film Festival, and Sundance Institute.
