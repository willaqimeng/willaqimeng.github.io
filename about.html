WILLA 洪浩齐萌
CUTHRELL-TUTTLEMAN 

Willa is an actor, writer, director, and visual artist. She was born in Jiangxi, China and grew up in New York City.
She holds a BA in English from Barnard College, where she graduated with honors in 2020. Her fiction has won the Axinn Foundation/Anna Quindlen Award for Creative Writing and has been published in the Columbia Daily Spectator and Asian American Writers' Workshop.
She is currently based in Los Angeles earning an MFA in Film & TV Production at USC's School of Cinematic Arts.
